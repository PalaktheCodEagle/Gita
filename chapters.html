Sacred Chapters of the Bhagavad Gita

1
Arjuna Vishada Yoga
(पहला अध्याय- अर्जुनविषादयोग)

The despondency of Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

2
Sankhya Yoga
(दूसरा अध्याय- सांख्ययोग)

The path of wisdom-distinguishing between the eternal soul and the temporary body.

3
Karma Yoga
(तीसरा अध्याय- कर्मयोग)

The path of selfless action and duty without attachment to results.

4
Jnana-Karma-Sanyasa Yoga
(चौथा अध्याय- ज्ञानकर्मसंन्यासयोग)

Wisdom in action-balancing knowledge and selfless work.

5
Karma-Sanyasa Yoga
(पाँचवा अध्याय- कर्मसंन्यासयोग)

Renunciation vs selfless action-choosing the higher path of duty.

6
Dhyana Yoga
(छठा अध्याय- आत्मसंयमयोग)

The path of meditation-controlling mind and senses to realize the self.

7
Jnana-Vijnana Yoga
(सातवाँ अध्याय- ज्ञानविज्ञानयोग)

Knowledge of the ultimate truth and divine wisdom of the Supreme.

8
Aksara Brahma Yoga
(आठवां अध्याय- अक्षरब्रह्मयोग)

Understanding the eternal imperishable self and the supreme reality.

9
Raja Vidya Raja Guhya Yoga
(नवाँ अध्याय- राजविद्याराजगुह्ययोग)

The supreme knowledge and the greatest secret revealed by Krishna.

10
Vibhuti Yoga
(दसवाँ अध्याय- विभूतियोग)

The divine glories of the Lord pervading the universe.

11
Vishwaroopa Darshana Yoga
(ग्यारहवाँ अध्याय- विश्वरूपदर्शनयोग)

The cosmic vision-Arjuna beholds Krishna's universal form.

12
Bhakti Yoga
(बारहवाँ अध्याय- भक्तियोग)

The path of love and devotion towards the Divine.

13
Ksetra-Ksetrajna Vibhaga Yoga
(तेरहवाँ अध्याय- क्षेत्र-क्षेत्रज्ञविभागयोग)

Distinction between body (field) and soul (knower of the field).

14
Gunatraya Vibhaga Yoga
(चौदहवाँ अध्याय- गुणत्रयविभागयोग)

The three modes of material nature-sattva, rajas, and tamas.

15
Purushottama Yoga
(पंद्रहवां अध्याय- पुरुषोत्तमयोग)

The Supreme Person beyond the perishable and imperishable.

16
Daivasura Sampad Vibhaga Yoga
(सोलहवाँ अध्याय- दैवासुरसम्पद्विभागयोग)

The divine and demoniac natures within human beings.

17
Sraddhatraya Vibhaga Yoga
(सत्रहवाँ अध्याय- श्रद्धात्रयविभागयोग)

The threefold division of faith influenced by nature's modes.

18
Moksha-Sanyasa Yoga
(अट्ठारहवाँ अध्याय- मोक्षसंन्यासयोग)

The path of liberation-renunciation and surrender to the Supreme.
