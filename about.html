About This Website

This website is a humble attempt to present the timeless wisdom of the Shrimad Bhagavad Geeta in a simple and accessible way.

My Inspiration

This project is lovingly dedicated to my Grandmother, whose devotion and faith inspired me to create a digital space where the sacred teachings of Lord Krishna can be experienced anytime, anywhere.

The Idea & Reason

In today’s fast-moving digital age, I wanted to bring the eternal knowledge of the Bhagavad Geeta closer to people through a modern yet simple platform. The idea is to make these teachings more approachable, especially for the younger generation, while keeping the sacredness intact.
